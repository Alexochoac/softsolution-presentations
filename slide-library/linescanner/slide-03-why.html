The Case for Inspection

Why inspection automation makes sense

⚠️ Without Inspection Automation
    Human Limitations
  • Operator fatigue — detection rates drop significantly over long shifts
  • As line speed increases, operator effectiveness declines — higher throughput means more defects slip through undetected 📈
  • 100% manual inspection is impossible at production speed
  • Distortion & anisotropy are invisible to the naked eye — no matter how skilled the operator
    • Business Impact
  • Inconsistent quality & frequent customer complaints
  • Construction site rejections — glass already delivered & installed means transport, rework & project delays 💰
  • Automotive: one defect triggers a full batch return — shipping & material costs on the entire load 💰
  • Increased liability risks & reputational damage
  • New item
✓ LineScanner & Osprey
  • 100% inspection of every glass, every shift — no fatigue, no blind spots
  • Real-time process control & quality feedback
  • Prove quality to customers — a documented defense against claims
  • Database & archive for shift accountability and CpK analysis
  • Acceptance procedures for new machinery (e.g. furnaces)
  • Increase throughput & reduce costly downstream rejects
  • Mandatory as per industry standards
  • Detects distortion, anisotropy & surface defects invisible to the human eye
