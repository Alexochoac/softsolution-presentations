# Customer Profile — SoftSolution

## About
SoftSolution is a B2B company using HubSpot as their core CRM and sales tool. They have a sales team operating across multiple European markets (Belgium, Italy, Luxembourg, Netherlands, Portugal, Spain).

## Audience
- B2B sales professionals (not technical)
- Use HubSpot daily for sequences, contacts, pipelines
- Teams managed by sales managers who track performance
- Multilingual team — keep language clear and simple
